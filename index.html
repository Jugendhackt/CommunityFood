Problems And Our Solution

We are a volunteer based organization that collects the surplus food from the restaurants, weddings, parties etc. to serve the less fortunate people . It is a zero funds organization in which volunteers contact the restaurants to donate the leftover food for the day which is then transported to the already set Community Kitchens. The under privileged people can then approach the Community Kitchens to acquire their share of food.
The challenge is not a lack of food — it is making food consistently available to everyone who needs it.
